About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli, the modern-day cricket legend, is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Kohli has consistently mesmerized cricket fans with his exceptional batting skills and remarkable leadership.

Kohli made his international debut for India in 2008 and quickly rose through the ranks to become the backbone of the Indian cricket team. His aggressive yet technically sound batting style has earned him numerous accolades and records in all formats of the game.

As the captain of the Indian cricket team, Kohli has led his side to numerous victories, including historic series wins overseas. His passion, determination, and commitment to excellence continue to inspire millions of fans around the world.
